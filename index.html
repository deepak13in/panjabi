About Us

“What makes difference:
We provide personal experience for to each client adhering to their own needs. Everything is planned according to the artist keeping in mind the originality and authenticity of their respective songs. Opinions and discussions are highly valued at Punjabi Cops.”

Our owner was fascinated with music from his childhood. His father bought him a piano when he was 5 years old. Since then he was unstoppable. He used to participate in various gatherings and competitions showcasing his music. When he was 16, he started exploring different online music platforms. He learned and displayed his skills locally and then was found by a company to work as a collaborator on some Music projects.
